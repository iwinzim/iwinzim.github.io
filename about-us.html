about Iwin

The Islamic Women’s Initiative Network (iWin) is an organisation established and managed by a group of like-minded women who embody the Islamic principals of peace, tolerance, integrity, compassion and humanity. 

The members of iWin are guided by excellence in all that they do, and it is their desire to make a positive difference by helping communities strengthen themselves.

iWin is an Islamic charity organisation dedicated to empowering the disadvantaged, women, young people and their families. iWin aims to assist women and youth to restore their lives and to provide appropriate opportunities for a better future.

Every act of kindness is charity

OUR MISSION

To promote, support and implement livelihood initiatives for vulnerable and disadvantaged communities.

To provide community development programmes for women and youth through education.
